About Us

Welcome to The Charity of Srdce Slovenska a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of individuals and communities across Slovakia. We are passionate about creating a brighter future for those in need, providing support and fostering a spirit of solidarity and kindness.

Our Aim

At Srdce Slovenska Charity our goal is to provide essential assistance to vulnerable groups in Slovakia. We are experts in alleviating poverty, improving access to education, supplementing healthcare, and promoting social inclusion. We mobilize local communities, partners, and volunteers to deliver sustainable solutions to the root causes of social problems.
